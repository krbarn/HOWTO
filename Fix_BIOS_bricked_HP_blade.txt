1. Power-off and remove the server from the enclosure 
2. Remove the lid 
3. Turn ON bits 1, 5 and 6 of the maintenance switch 
4. Replace the lid 
5. Reinstall the server 
6. Power on and wait until you hear the beep - should take a few seconds.
7. Power-off and remove the server from the enclosure 
8. Remove the lid 
9. Turn OFF bits 1, 5 and 6 of the maintenance switch 
10. Replace the lid 
11. Reinstall and power on the server
